What's speech act? (Levinson 1983)文献总结

四月 22, 2020 语言学 本文总阅读量次

作者信息

Stephen C.Levinson is a British linguist, educated at Cambridge in the UK, and UCB in the USA, known for his studies of pragmatics and the relationship between human language and cognition. He is currently one of the scientific directors of the MPI for Psycholinguistics in Nijmegen.

文本信息

The following literature is the fifth chapter of a textbook Pragmatics, which is the first comprehensive textbook in this field (1983). This book is based on the work of Gricean conversation implicatures and Austin’s speech acts, contains the most important aspects of pragmatic: deixis, implicatures, presupposition and speech acts.
